Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

IZZA LLC respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when using Izza and related services. We aim to collect only the information needed to provide and improve the app experience.

Information we may collect

Depending on the features used, Izza may process information related to app settings, workout entries, habit progress, prayer preferences, audio playback preferences, and support requests.

Fitness and health information

If health or workout integrations are enabled, Izza may request permission to access relevant fitness data only after the user grants permission through iOS. Users can manage these permissions at any time in iOS settings.

Location information

Izza may request location access for features such as Qibla direction or prayer-related tools. Location permissions are controlled by the user through iOS settings.

How information is used

Information is used to operate app features, improve reliability, respond to support requests, and maintain a safe and useful user experience. IZZA LLC does not sell personal information.
